How many Ukrainians are there in the U.S.?

There are at least 1,000,000 people in the U.S. that either have Ukrainian ancestry or are Ukrainian-born themselves1. This is more than 4x times more than in Germany.

Is that a lot?

Imagine, if just 1 out of 100 of all the Ukrainians in the U.S. attends this meeting, that would be 10,000 people — enough to get the necessary attention to the issue.
